Hi, I'm Darshan Thaker

I'm a 2nd year Masters Student studying Computer Science at Columbia University. I graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science and Pure Mathematics from the University of Texas at Austin, where I was part of the Turing Scholars honors program.

My research interests are in theoretical machine learning, with a focus on the theory of neural networks, non-convex optimization, and learning theory. I am fortunate to have been advised during my MS by Prof. John Wright.

Outside of my academic interests, I enjoy playing the piano to learn how to popular songs. I also love bouldering and have been doing a lot of that recently.
